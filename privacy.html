Our game and website may use third-party services that collect information about you. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

Children's Privacy

Count Master 3D Game is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
